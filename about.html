Our Story

Supplying Nairobi Since Day One

Milestone Creative Solutions Limited is a leading provider of innovative and tailored creative supply solutions with a focus on meeting the diverse needs of creative professionals, institutions and businesses.

We specialize in sourcing, distributing and delivering a wide range of high quality supplies essential for various creative endeavors and needs across Nairobi and beyond.

From our base in Nairobi, we serve schools, hospitals, businesses and individual creators — delivering everything from bulk exercise books to fully equipped science labs.
